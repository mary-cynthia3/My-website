Stages of Education

Nursery

This is the first stage of formal education. Children are thought how to speak and communicate with people, also the basics of English Language and arithmetic are introduced

primary

It is believed that at this stage, all should be able to communicate to some extent and are also familiar with the basics of English Language. Therefore, a higher approach is implemented in the learning process. Pupils are exposed to more adcanced tasks.

Secondary

The mind is more matured and so, it is expected to take into cognisense broader and higher knowledge, it is expected to try to understand solve situations.

University

A school of advanced knowledge learning to create solutions to life problems in different ways.
